Amazon Sales Dashboard Analysis

📊 Project Overview

This repository contains the analysis and visualization logic for our Amazon Sales performance. The goal is to provide stakeholders with actionable insights regarding revenue trends, category performance, and customer segmentation to drive profitable growth.

🚀 Key Metrics Analyzed

Total Revenue & Growth Trends: Analyzing performance across quarters.

Conversion Rates: Identifying the efficiency of top-performing SKUs.

Profitability by SKU: Determining actual margins after FBA fees, COGS, and ad spend.

TACoS (Total Advertising Cost of Sales): Measuring marketing efficiency.

Customer Segmentation: Evaluating AOV and repeat purchase behavior.

🛠 Tech Stack

Data Processing: Excel.

Visualization: Excel Charts .

Data Source: Amazon Seller Central API / Exported CSV.

📈 Insights Summary

Our analysis revealed that 20% of products account for 80% of our profit margin (Pareto Principle).

Seasonality significantly impacts our Electronics category, requiring more aggressive inventory planning in Q4.

Customer retention remains the largest opportunity for reducing CPA.
